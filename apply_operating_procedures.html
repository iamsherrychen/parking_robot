After the landowner completes the venue application on
the website, we will arrange the robot to the site to
evaluate whether the venue can be used for parking, and
the apply status displays "In the process".

will be returned to the website, the apply status will display
"success"; if the relevant conditions are not met, the
application will be cancelled, the apply status will display
"canceled".
