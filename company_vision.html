Due to the limited land space in the city, parking space is
generally insufficient in Taipei City, and it is not easy to
obtain parking land. Proper use of land is relatively important.
Many urban planners often struggle with the problem of
insufficient parking spaces, but effective land use is even more
important.
Company
Vision
In order to make effective use of idle space, we hope to diversify the use of urban integration
areas, open spaces after the demolition of old houses, transitional areas created by the
conversion of new and old buildings, and current parking lots...etc.

The land owner can use the space flexibly as a venue for various activities such as fairs, music
festivals, and community gatherings. When the land is not in use, it can be used as a parking
lot, and the idle parking resources can be redistributed to achieve a multi-use area. Economic
benefits.

a livable city.
