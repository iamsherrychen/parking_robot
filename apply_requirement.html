01
After landowner completes the venue
application on the website, we will arrange
the robot to the site to evaluate whether the
venue can be used for parking, and apply
status will displays "In the process".
02
If the relevant conditions are met, the evaluation
result will be returned to website, apply status
will display "success"; if the relevant conditions
are not met, the application will be cancelled,
apply status will display "canceled".
03
After robot evaluation, you can view the
parking space plan on the website. If you
want to modify the site plan, you can call
0800-xxx-xxx and we will adjust the site
configuration for you.
04
If you agree to the final planning result, our
company will handle the parking lot usage
procedures for you.
05
You can adjust the site "provide parking
service" at any time on the website. If you
have other site usage needs, you can
temporarily stop using it as a parking lot,
and open the parking lot when the site has
no other uses.
